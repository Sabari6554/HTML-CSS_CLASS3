Over View of the vehicle

Pulsar-the sports bike that gives every aspiring rider the power to play. The bike that answers the need to seek thrill and adventure every single day. A bike that has become a symbol of the bikers individuality

A technological breakthrough, Triple Spark Ignition upgrades the concept of twin spark even further. Three spark plugs are employed to provide best in class performance and efficiency.

T.E.C in ExhausTEC stands for Torque Expansion Chamber - a tuned box or a resonator optimally and unobtrusively placed in the exhaust system of the vehicle to provide more torque in the low to mid-range RPMs of the engine.

This essentially uses 2 or 3 spark plugs in the cylinder head coupled with a digital ignition system to provide lean burn characteristics and faster combustion that results in more power and torque while guaranteeing improved mileage.

Intelligent C.D.I works along with TRICS III to provide optimum ignition timings at different engine RPMs creating a ride thats smooth, noise free and fuel efficient.
